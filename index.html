Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I enroll in an Odoo training course?
Enrolling is simple! Just visit our Odoo Training page, choose the course that fits your business or technical goals, and register online. Once enrolled, you'll receive a confirmation email with your schedule and access instructions.
What are the prerequisites for Odoo training?
No strict prerequisites! However, having a basic understanding of business operations (like sales, accounting, or inventory) helps. For our technical Odoo training, some familiarity with Python programming and PostgreSQL is recommended.
How do I get support during the training?
We provide dedicated trainer support via live chat, email, and virtual Q&A sessions. You’ll also get access to our resource library and a private learner community for ongoing help.
Are live training sessions recorded?
Yes, all live Odoo sessions are recorded. You’ll receive video replays after each class so you can review them anytime, at your own pace — perfect for revision or catching up if you miss a session.
Will I get a certificate after completing the course?
Absolutely! Once you complete your training, you’ll receive a recognized Certificate of Completion endorsed by our team of Odoo experts — a valuable addition to your professional portfolio.
